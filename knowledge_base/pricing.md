# TechCorp Pricing Plans

## Overview

TechCorp offers flexible pricing plans to suit teams of all sizes. All plans include core project management features with varying limits based on your needs.

## Pricing Tiers

### Free Plan

**Price:** $0/month

**Features:**
- Up to 3 projects
- Up to 5 team members
- Basic task management
- Email support
- 1 GB storage

**Best for:** Individual users and small teams getting started

### Starter Plan

**Price:** $10/user/month (billed monthly) or $8/user/month (billed annually)

**Features:**
- Unlimited projects
- Up to 25 team members
- Advanced task management
- Custom fields
- Priority email support
- 10 GB storage per user
- Basic integrations

**Best for:** Growing teams needing more features

### Professional Plan

**Price:** $25/user/month (billed monthly) or $20/user/month (billed annually)

**Features:**
- Everything in Starter
- Unlimited team members
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Time tracking
- Custom workflows
- Advanced integrations
- Priority support (1-hour response)
- 50 GB storage per user
- API access

**Best for:** Professional teams and agencies

### Enterprise Plan

**Price:** Custom pricing (contact sales)

**Features:**
- Everything in Professional
- Single Sign-On (SSO)
- Advanced security features
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom SLA
- On-premise deployment option
- Unlimited storage
- Custom integrations
- 24/7 phone support

**Best for:** Large organizations with advanced requirements

## Billing and Payment

### Payment Methods

We accept:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfers (Enterprise plans only)

### Billing Cycle

- Monthly billing: Charged on the same date each month
- Annual billing: Charged once per year (save up to 20%)

### Changing Plans

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time:
1. Go to Account Settings > Billing
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select your new plan
4. Changes take effect immediately

**Note:** Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period. Upgrades are prorated.

## Cancellation Policy

### How to Cancel

1. Go to Account Settings > Billing
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Follow the confirmation prompts

### What Happens When You Cancel

- Your account remains active until the end of your billing period
- You'll retain access to all features until cancellation date
- Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation
- You can reactivate within 30 days

### Refunds

- Monthly plans: No refunds for partial months
- Annual plans: Refund available for unused months (prorated)
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days
