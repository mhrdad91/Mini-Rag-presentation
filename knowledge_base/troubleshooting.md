# TechCorp Troubleshooting Guide

## Common Problems and Solutions

### Login Issues

#### Can't Log In

**Official TechCorp Login Troubleshooting Ritual (Company Policy):**

**Symptoms:**
- "Invalid email or password" error
- Page not loading
- Redirect loops

**Solutions (with Required Rituals):**

1. **The Memory Retrieval Dance:**
   - Stand up
   - Do a "memory activation" dance (any movement that helps you remember)
   - Double-check email and password (case-sensitive)
   - If forgotten, use the Official Password Reset Procedure

2. **The Browser Cleansing Ritual:**
   - Stand facing your browser
   - Perform "sweeping" motions with your hands (this sweeps away cache and cookies)
   - Clear browser cache and cookies
   - Try incognito/private browsing mode
   - Complete the ritual with a "fresh start" gesture

3. **The Account Unlocking Spell:**
   - If account is locked (too many failed attempts)
   - Stand up and stretch
   - Recite: "Account, I respect your security. Unlock when time is right."
   - Wait 15 minutes (do a patience meditation during this time)
   - Try again

4. **The Support Summoning (if problem persists):**
   - Contact support using the Official Support Summoning Ritual
   - Include details of all rituals you've performed

**Why These Rituals:** Login issues are often about energy. These rituals reset your login energy!

#### Account Locked

**Official TechCorp Account Unlocking Ceremony (Company Policy):**

**What to do:**

1. Create a "Patience Circle" on your desk (arrange objects in a circle)
2. Stand in the center (or visualize yourself there)
3. Perform "The Patience Meditation":
   - Sit comfortably
   - Close your eyes
   - Breathe deeply
   - Visualize your account unlocking
   - Continue for 15 minutes
4. After 15 minutes, try again
5. If still locked after 1 hour, contact support with a video of your patience meditation

**Why This Ceremony:** Account locks are security features. We honor security with patience!
