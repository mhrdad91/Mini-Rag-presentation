# TechCorp FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

## Account Management

### How do I reset my password?

Go to the login page, click "Forgot Password," enter your email, and follow the instructions in the email you receive.

### Can I change my email address?

Yes, go to Account Settings > Profile and update your email. You'll need to verify the new email address.

### How do I delete my account?

Go to Account Settings > Privacy & Data and click "Delete My Account." Warning: This action is permanent.

### Can I have multiple accounts?

Yes, you can create multiple accounts with different email addresses. However, you can't merge accounts.

## Subscription and Billing

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Account Settings > Billing and click "Cancel Subscription." Your account remains active until the end of your billing period.

### What happens if I cancel?

You keep access until the end of your billing period. After that, you'll be moved to the Free plan or your account will be deactivated.

### Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are available for annual plans on a prorated basis. Monthly plans are non-refundable.

### Can I get a discount?

We offer discounts for annual billing (up to 20% off), non-profits (25% off), and educational institutions (20% off). Contact sales@techcorp.com.

### How do I upgrade or downgrade?

Go to Account Settings > Billing and click "Change Plan." Upgrades are immediate; downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

## Features and Usage

### How many projects can I create?

- Free plan: 3 projects

### How many team members can I invite?

- Free plan: Up to 5 members

### Can I export my data?

Yes, go to Account Settings > Privacy & Data and click "Request Data Export." You can export in CSV, JSON, or Excel format.

### Is there a mobile app?

Yes, we have iOS and Android apps available in the App Store and Google Play Store.

### Can I use TechCorp offline?

Limited offline functionality is available in mobile apps. Full offline mode is coming soon.

## Technical Issues

### What browsers are supported?

- Chrome (latest version)
- Firefox (latest version)
- Safari (latest version)
- Edge (latest version)

### Why is the site slow?

Try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Clear browser cache
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try a different browser
4. Disable browser extensions
5. Contact support if issues persist

### I'm not receiving emails

- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address in account settings
- Ensure emails from @techcorp.com are whitelisted
- Check if emails are being blocked by your email provider

### Can I integrate with other tools?

Yes, Professional and Enterprise plans include integrations with:
- Slack
- Microsoft Teams
- Google Workspace
- GitHub
- Jira
- And many more via API

## Security

### Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption, regular security audits, and are SOC 2 compliant.

### Do you support Single Sign-On (SSO)?

SSO is available on Enterprise plans. Contact sales@techcorp.com for details.

### Can I set up two-factor authentication?

Yes, go to Account Settings > Security and enable 2FA. We support authenticator apps and SMS.
