# TechCorp Data Export and Privacy

## Data Export

### Exporting Your Data

You can export all your data at any time:

1. Go to Account Settings > Privacy & Data
2. Click "Request Data Export"
3. Select data types to export:
   - Projects and tasks
   - Team member information
   - Comments and attachments
   - Analytics and reports
4. Choose export format (CSV, JSON, or Excel)
5. Click "Generate Export"
6. You'll receive an email when export is ready (usually within 24 hours)
7. Download link expires after 7 days

### Export Formats

- **CSV:** Best for spreadsheets and data analysis
- **JSON:** Best for developers and integrations
- **Excel:** Best for complex reports with multiple sheets

### What's Included in Exports

- All projects and tasks
- Team member lists and roles
- Comments and activity logs
- File attachments (links to cloud storage)
- Project templates
- Custom fields and configurations

**Note:** Exported attachments are links to files stored in TechCorp. You'll need to download files separately if needed.

## Account Deletion

### How to Delete Your Account

1. Go to Account Settings > Privacy & Data
2. Scroll to "Delete Account"
3. Click "Delete My Account"
4. Enter your password to confirm
5. Type "DELETE" to confirm
6. Click "Permanently Delete"

**Warning:** Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

### What Happens When You Delete

- All your data is permanently deleted
- You'll lose access to all projects
- Team members you invited remain in their own workspaces
- Projects you created may be transferred to workspace admin
- Billing is cancelled immediately
- No refunds for unused time

### Data Retention After Deletion

- Account data: Deleted immediately
- Backup data: Deleted within 30 days
- Logs and analytics: Anonymized within 90 days

- Right to object
