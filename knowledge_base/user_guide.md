# TechCorp Project Management Platform - User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to TechCorp! Our project management platform helps teams collaborate more effectively.

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit our website and click "Sign Up"
2. Enter your email address
3. Choose a secure password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile setup

### First Steps After Signup

- Set up your workspace
- Invite team members
- Create your first project
- Explore the dashboard

## Managing Your Account

### Password Management

#### Resetting Your Password

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset instructions
5. Click the reset link and create a new password

**Note:** Password reset links expire after 24 hours for security.

#### Changing Your Password

To change your password while logged in:

1. Click your profile icon (top right)
2. Select "Account Settings"
3. Navigate to "Security"
4. Click "Change Password"
5. Enter your current password and new password
6. Save changes

### Profile Settings

Update your profile information, notification preferences, and privacy settings in Account Settings.

## Collaboration Features

### Inviting Team Members

To invite team members to your workspace:

1. Navigate to "Team" in the sidebar
2. Click "Invite Members"
3. Enter email addresses (separate multiple emails with commas)
4. Select their role (Admin, Member, Viewer)
5. Click "Send Invitations"

Invited members will receive an email with setup instructions.

### Project Permissions

- **Admin:** Full access to all features
- **Member:** Can create and edit projects
- **Viewer:** Read-only access

## Projects and Tasks

### Creating a Project

1. Click "New Project" button
2. Enter project name and description
3. Set start and end dates (optional)
4. Choose a project template (optional)
5. Click "Create"

### Adding Tasks

1. Open your project
2. Click "Add Task"
3. Enter task details
4. Assign to team members
5. Set due dates and priorities

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Can't log in?**
- Check your email and password
- Try resetting your password
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Contact support if issues persist

**Not receiving emails?**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address in account settings
- Ensure emails from @techcorp.com are whitelisted

**Slow performance?**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
